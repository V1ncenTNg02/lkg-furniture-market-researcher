# Sector Overview

description: Optional helper for summarising the Australian bedding, mattresses, sleep products, and bedroom furniture sector. Use when the user asks for sector background, market structure, key players, demand drivers, or a board-pack primer.

## Purpose

Create a concise sector overview that supports the weekly digest. This is not a valuation exercise.

## Coverage

Include:

- Market structure and main channels: specialty bedding retailers, furniture chains, department/large-format retailers, online marketplaces, and direct-to-consumer mattress brands.
- LKG-relevant operating context: Hypnos Group, Snooze, Future Sleep, and G&G Furniture.
- Competitors: Forty Winks, Bedshed, Sleeping Giant, IKEA Australia, Harvey Norman, Domayne, Temple & Webster, Adairs, Nick Scali, Freedom, Fantastic Furniture, and Amart Furniture.
- Demand drivers: housing turnover, renovation activity, consumer confidence, discretionary spending, cost-of-living pressure, and retail trade trends.
- Margin drivers: freight, FX, raw materials, promotional intensity, inventory, and manufacturing/import conditions.

## Rules

- Use public sources only.
- Cite claims with URLs.
- Avoid unsourced market-size estimates.
- Flag stale or uncertain data.
