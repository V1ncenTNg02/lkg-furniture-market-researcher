# Competitive Analysis

## Purpose

Build a practical competitor view for the LKG weekly market digest. The goal is to identify actions that matter for Snooze, Hypnos Group, Future Sleep, G&G Furniture, portfolio company GMs, or the LKG board.

## Competitor Set

Default competitors:

- Forty Winks.
- Bedshed.
- Sleeping Giant.
- IKEA Australia.
- Harvey Norman.
- Domayne.
- Temple & Webster.
- Adairs.
- Nick Scali.
- Freedom.
- Fantastic Furniture.
- Amart Furniture.

## What To Compare

Track:

- Promotions and discounting.
- Mattress, bed frame, bedroom suite, and sleep-product range changes.
- Delivery, installation, financing, and warranty offers.
- Store openings, closures, refurbishments, or franchise signals.
- Online user experience, merchandising, and product availability signals.
- Public trading updates and investor commentary where available.


## Rules

- Do not scrape private or restricted systems.
- Use public information only.
- Treat competitor pages as evidence of current offers, not as proof of sales performance.
- Separate observed facts from interpretation.
