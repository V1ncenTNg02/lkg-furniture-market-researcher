# Weekly Digest Template

description: Writes a concise weekly market digest from structured research findings for LKG's bedding and bedroom furniture monitoring workflow.

## Output Contract

Create a draft digest suitable for Word, email, PowerPoint outline, or Markdown. The default is a Word-style memo.

Use this structure:

```text
# LKG Bedding and Bedroom Furniture Market Digest

Period:
Prepared for:
Status: Draft for human review

## Executive Summary
- 5-8 bullets covering the most important signals.

## Key Market Signals
| Date | Company / Sector | What happened | Why this matters | Audience | Impact area | Risk level | Confidence | Human review |

## Competitor Watch
- Promotions, pricing, store moves, product launches, delivery/financing offers.

## Demand And Consumer Trends
- Retail trade, housing, consumer confidence, cost-of-living pressure, discretionary spending.

## Supply Chain And Margin Signals
- Freight, FX, raw materials, imports, inventory, manufacturing, supplier disruption.

## Items For Portfolio Company GM
- Operationally actionable items.

## Items For LKG Board
- Strategic, financial, risk, M&A, or reputation items.

## Items Requiring Human Review
- Low-confidence, high-impact, sensitive, or interpretive items.

## Source Log
| Source | URL | Date accessed | Evidence used | Reliability |

## Disclaimer
Draft work product only. Public data only. Not financial advice. Human approval required before circulation.
```

## Writing Rules

- Be concise and practical.
- Every item must include "why this matters."
- Keep "what happened" factual.
- Keep "why this matters" clearly interpretive.
- Preserve source links, confidence, and human-review flags.
- Do not invent missing evidence.
- Do not send, email, or publish.
