EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Harvey Norman posted a 6.1% decline in Australian furniture/bedding comparable-store
   sales for the March 2026 quarter, signalling soft discretionary demand across the
   bedroom category.

2. Forty Winks launched a national 'Sleep Better' promotional campaign (up to 40% off
   mattresses and bases) active through end of May 2026, intensifying promotional
   competition.

3. Temple & Webster reported 18% revenue growth for Q3 FY2026, driven by online mattress
   and bedroom furniture demand; online channel shift is accelerating.

4. IKEA Australia announced a new 10,000 sqm store opening in Marsden Park (western
   Sydney) for Q3 2026, expanding bedroom and sleep-product competition in NSW.

5. ABS Retail Trade data (March 2026) shows household goods retailing fell 1.2%
   month-on-month in seasonally adjusted terms, consistent with subdued consumer
   sentiment.
